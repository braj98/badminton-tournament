Tournament Manager Platform - Product & Technical Roadmap
Vision

Build a lightweight, reusable tournament management platform for apartment communities, clubs, schools, and local events.

The platform should support multiple sports while remaining easy to deploy, inexpensive to host, and simple to operate.

The system is not intended to be a permanent historical archive.

Data retention goal:

Current Year
Previous Year

Older tournament data may be archived or deleted.

What Not To Do

Do not add:

Multiple sports
Player statistics
Historical analytics
Notifications
WhatsApp integration
Referee management

until Phase 1 is complete.

Every new feature before stabilization increases bug count and slows progress.
