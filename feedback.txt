But I See One Limitation

Suppose tomorrow you create

Senior Boys

Oops.

Collision.

Now you cannot keep:

2026

2027

simultaneously.

I Think This Is Your Biggest Remaining Data Model Issue

Right now your key is essentially

Sport
+
Competition

It is missing Event.

Instead I would eventually move to

btm_state_<eventId>_<competitionId>

Example

btm_state_sportsmeet2026_seniorboys

btm_state_sportsmeet2027_seniorboys

or even

btm_state_evt123_cmp456

Much safer.

My Recommendation

I would not change anything today.

Seriously.

Because changing every key now will touch almost every part of your application.

Instead I'd add this to your backlog:

Future Migration (V2)

Current:

btm_state_<competitionName>

Future:

btm_state_<eventId>_<competitionId>

This will support:

Multiple events
Multiple sports
Historical tournaments
Duplicate competition names
Multi-organization

without any collisions.
