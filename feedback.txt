Think About The User

There are two very different users.

User 1 - Resident / Spectator

They don't care about:

Senior Doubles only

They care about:

What is happening in the tournament right now?

For them:

🔥 Live

should show all live matches across all categories.

🏸 Senior Doubles
Court 1

Amit/Rajesh
vs

🏸 Junior Singles

Rahul

This is the tournament dashboard.

User 2 - Player

A player in:

Senior Doubles

doesn't care about:

Junior Singles

They care about:

their category

So when they enter:

Senior Doubles

they should see:

🔥 Live
📅 Upcoming
📖 Results
🏆 Champion

filtered to Senior Doubles.
